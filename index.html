Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025

This application does not collect, process, or transmit any personal data from users. All passwords generated and stored by the application remain strictly local on the user's device. The app does not connect to any external servers or third-party services.

Data Collection

No personal or technical data is collected. The application operates fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data Transmission

No data is transmitted outside the device. The app does not use cloud services, analytics tools, advertising, or third-party integrations.

Data Storage

All user-generated data (such as passwords) is stored locally on the device. Users have full control over their data and can delete it at any time from within the app or by uninstalling the app.

User Rights

In accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users have the right to:

  • Know what data is stored (in this case, only data they themselves generated);
  • Delete their data at any time;
  • Use the app without submitting any personal information.
